## Overall status

Philharmonic is in active implementation with design largely settled. The workspace README now records Phase 6 as complete and Phase 7 Tier 1 as in progress: the connector triangle and foundational crates are published, and Phase 7 Tier 1 wave 1 shipped `philharmonic-connector-impl-sql-postgres`, `philharmonic-connector-impl-sql-mysql`, and `philharmonic-connector-impl-vector-search` version `0.1.0` on 2026-04-27, while `philharmonic-connector-impl-embed` remains unpublished during its rewrite from `fastembed`/`ort` to `tract`/`tokenizers`.

## What is next

Per the roadmap, the immediate next Phase 7 task is the tract-based rewrite of `philharmonic-connector-impl-embed`, after which Tier 1 wave 2 can ship. After Tier 1 is complete, the next planned connector is `philharmonic-connector-impl-email-smtp` (Tier 2). Tier 3 work on `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-anthropic` and `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-gemini` is explicitly deferred until on or after 2026-05-07.

## Notable concerns or blockers

- `philharmonic-connector-impl-embed` is blocked from publication by the library/runtime pivot: the original `fastembed` + `ort` approach was held back because the `ort-download-binaries` path imposed a glibc-only linking constraint, while deployment targets include musl. The tract/tokenizers rewrite is therefore the current prerequisite for completing Phase 7 Tier 1.
- The 2026-04-27 wave-1 publish commit notes that some ambiguities from the 2026-04-26 planning note remain open ahead of the embed tract Codex dispatch (`158001c`).
