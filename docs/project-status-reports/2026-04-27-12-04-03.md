The workspace README and roadmap now agree that Phase 6 is complete and Phase 7 Tier 1 is complete end-to-end: the connector foundation, first implementation set, and all Tier 1 data-layer connectors are published, including the `embed` crate and the supporting `inline-blob` crate on 2026-04-27.
