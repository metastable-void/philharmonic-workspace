## Overall status

Philharmonic is in active Phase 9 integration work with the foundational crates, connector triangle, Phase 7 Tier 1 implementations, and Phase 8 API already completed and published where planned. As of 2026-04-30, the workspace has the meta-crate wiring, all three bin targets, embedded WebUI, end-to-end MySQL testcontainer coverage, and the real lowerer/executor path landed; the remaining roadmap focus is publishing the meta-crate and finishing post-happy-path deployment work.

## What is next

Per `ROADMAP.md`, the immediate next work is the remaining Phase 9 items after the happy-path integration milestone: publish the `philharmonic` meta-crate once the remaining optional implementation placeholders are publishable, then tackle the deferrable deployment tasks in order—`install` subcommands, musl-target verification in CI, reference deployment, Docker Compose, and finally documentation reconciliation. Separately, outside the Phase 9 critical path, Phase 7 Tier 2/3 still leaves SMTP plus Anthropic and Gemini connector implementations to ship, with the additional LLM providers explicitly deferred until after 2026-05-07.

## Notable concerns or blockers

- The roadmap says publishing `philharmonic` 0.1.0 depends on first publishing the three still-unshipped optional implementation crates (`philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-anthropic`, `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-gemini`, `philharmonic-connector-impl-email-smtp`); this is the stated prerequisite for Phase 9 task 1 completion.
- The reference-deployment path depends on the newly landed real lowerer and executor work; although tasks 7–8 are now marked done, the roadmap still distinguishes deployment-grade follow-through (musl, install flow, real deployment, doc reconciliation) as remaining work.
