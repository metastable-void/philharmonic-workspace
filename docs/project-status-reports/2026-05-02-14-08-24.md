## Overall status

Philharmonic is past its v1 implementation path: design is settled, Phases 0–9 are complete, and the reference deployment is operational as of 2026-05-02, including a verified end-to-end workflow run from the WebUI through the API server, mechanics worker, connector router/service, and an OpenAI-compatible upstream LLM.

## What is next

Per `ROADMAP.md` §9, active work is now post-v1: first priority is the embedding-datasets track, starting with the Gate-1 lowerer-ephemeral proposal and independent groundwork such as the MySQL `LONGBLOB` migration and optional mechanics job timeout support. In parallel or shortly after, the remaining substantive connector implementations (`email-smtp`, `llm-anthropic`, `llm-gemini`) and WebUI/docs follow-through—especially a maintained in-browser code editor and a rewrite of the workflow authoring guide—remain planned.

## Notable concerns or blockers

- Embedding-datasets dispatches D4 and D5 are explicitly blocked on the new Gate-1 crypto-sensitive proposal for lowerer ephemeral support; the roadmap states they cannot start until that review clears.
- The remaining Tier 2/3 connector crates are still placeholders at `0.0.x` until their substantive implementations land.
