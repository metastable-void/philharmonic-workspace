Philharmonic is in a strong post-v1 state: design is settled, the v1 path is complete through Phase 9, the reference deployment has been operational since 2026-05-02, and the main post-v1 feature wave on 2026-05-10 shipped embedding datasets, the chat-style WebUI test surface, the workflow authoring guide rewrite, and the `llm-openai-compat` custom-headers enhancement.

## What is next

Per `docs/ROADMAP.md`, the remaining post-v1 dispatches are D7, D8, and D9: the substantive Tier 2/3 connector implementations for SMTP, Anthropic, and Gemini. These are independent, parallel-safe, and represent the main unfinished post-v1 scope now that D1–D6 and D10–D13 are complete.

## Notable concerns or blockers

- The roadmap identifies no deployment blocker, but the remaining planned implementation work is concentrated in the three still-placeholder connector crates: `philharmonic-connector-impl-email-smtp`, `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-anthropic`, and `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-gemini`.
- A dependency audit still leaves one unresolved upstream vulnerability: `rsa 0.9.10` (`RUSTSEC-2023-0071`), which the `cargo update` pass could not clear because no newer published fix is available (`fcad83f`).
