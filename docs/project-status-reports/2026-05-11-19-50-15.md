Per `docs/ROADMAP.md`, the remaining active post-v1 work is the three Tier 2/3 connector implementations: D7 `philharmonic-connector-impl-email-smtp`, D8 `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-anthropic`, and D9 `philharmonic-connector-impl-llm-gemini`.
